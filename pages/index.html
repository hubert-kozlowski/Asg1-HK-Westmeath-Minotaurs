Welcome to the Westmeath Minotaurs

American Football is the fastest growing sport in Ireland for a reason. Intense, hard hitting, strategic, and challenging. There is no other team sport like it. On every play every player is critical to the success of the team.

All sizes, shapes, skill levels, and athletic backgrounds are welcome. There are 88 positions required on a team; all with unique requirements. We will find you a role; you will contribute. The Minotaurs staff will coach and develop your football skills from scratch to a high level. All you need to do is provide the commitment.
