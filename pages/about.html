Westmeath Minotaurs

About Us

The Westmeath Minotaurs are a dedicated American Football team based in County Westmeath, Ireland. Established in 2012, we have quickly grown to become one of the most competitive teams in the Irish American Football League (IAFL).

Our mission is to promote the sport of American Football in Ireland, providing opportunities for players of all skill levels to learn, grow, and compete. We pride ourselves on our strong community spirit, teamwork, and commitment to excellence both on and off the field.

Whether you're a seasoned player or new to the sport, we welcome you to join us and be part of our exciting journey. Together, we strive for success and aim to bring home championships while fostering a love for the game.
